Devon Larratt

The Canadian Hook Master

Devon Larratt is widely considered the greatest all-around armwrestler in the history of the sport. Born in Canada, Devon has dominated armwrestling for over two decades, winning multiple world championships across different weight classes and federations. His technical versatility, mental toughness, and ability to adapt to any opponent are legendary.

Background & Early Career

Devon Larratt was born on April 16, 1979, in New Westminster, British Columbia, Canada. He discovered armwrestling as a young man and immediately demonstrated exceptional talent. His early career saw him dominating local and regional competitions, quickly establishing himself as a rising star in the armwrestling world.

What set Devon apart from the beginning was his versatility. While many armwrestlers specialize in one technique, Devon mastered multiple techniques—the hook, toproll, side pressure, and king's move. This versatility became his signature.

Major Championships & Titles

  • Multiple World Championships: Devon has won world championships across different weight classes and in multiple federations (WAL, ARM, EJF).
  • Over 20 Years of Dominance: Since breaking into the professional scene in the early 2000s, Devon has remained competitive at the highest levels.
  • Evolution as an Athlete: As Devon aged, he evolved his technique and strategy, proving that intelligence and adaptation beat youth.
  • Multiple Top Rankings: Devon has consistently ranked in the top 3 heavyweights in the world for two decades.
  • Historic Matches: Devon has participated in some of the most memorable, epic armwrestling matches in history.

Technical Mastery

The Hook: Devon's hook is legendary. His forearm strength, wrist curl velocity, and crushing power have defeated countless opponents using the hook technique.

The Toproll: Devon's toproll is equally devastating. His shoulder positioning and leveraging ability allow him to execute powerful toprolls against any opponent.

Defensive Mastery: What truly separates Devon is his ability to defend against any technique. His hand control and positioning allow him to counter whatever his opponent attempts.

Adaptability: Devon is known for studying his opponents thoroughly, identifying weaknesses, and adjusting his strategy accordingly. This intellectual approach to armwrestling is part of what makes him great.

Mental Strength & Mindset

Beyond physical strength, Devon's mental toughness is exceptional. He approaches every match with complete confidence and preparation. His competitive mindset is legendary—he expects to win and trains accordingly.

Devon is also known for his sportsmanship and respect for opponents. Despite his dominance, he conducts himself with class and dignity, earning respect throughout the armwrestling community.

Contributions to the Sport

  • Elevated the Sport Globally: Devon's matches and personality helped bring armwrestling to a global audience.
  • Mentored New Competitors: Devon has helped train and mentor younger armwrestlers, contributing to the next generation.
  • Technical Innovation: Devon's techniques and strategies have been studied and imitated by countless armwrestlers.
  • Entertainment Value: Devon brings incredible energy to matches, making them entertaining for spectators and broadcasters.

Key Lessons from Devon's Career

  • Versatility is Strength: Master multiple techniques rather than relying on a single move.
  • Never Stop Improving: Devon continues to evolve his techniques even after 20+ years in the sport.
  • Mental Game Matters: Confidence, preparation, and mental toughness are as important as physical strength.
  • Study Your Opponents: Understanding your opponent's strengths and weaknesses allows you to create a winning strategy.
  • Longevity Through Adaptation: As you age, adaptation and technique become more important than raw physical power.
