Understanding Arm Positioning

Before diving into specific techniques, you must understand the basic arm positioning and hand placement that forms the foundation of all armwrestling moves. Every technique builds upon proper positioning at the table.

  • Hand Placement: Your hand must be palm-down, fingers wrapped firmly around your opponent's hand.
  • Elbow Position: Your elbow stays on the pad, maintaining contact and control throughout the match.
  • Shoulder Alignment: Proper shoulder positioning transfers power from your body to your arm.
  • Grip Strength: A crushing grip is essential for controlling your opponent and executing all techniques.

Progressive Learning Path

New to armwrestling? We recommend learning techniques in this order:

  1. Hook (Beginner-Friendly): Start with the hook as it's the most intuitive and popular technique.
  2. Toproll: Once comfortable with hooks, learn the toproll for versatility.
  3. Side Pressure: Build lateral pressure control.
  4. King's Move: Master the pressure-based approach.
  5. Tricep Press: Finally, perfect the pure strength method.

Remember: technique beats strength. A perfectly executed move will defeat raw power every time.
