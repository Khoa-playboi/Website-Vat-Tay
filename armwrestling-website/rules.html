Why Rules Matter

In armwrestling, rules aren't meant to limit competition‚Äîthey're meant to ensure fair play and safety. Understanding the rules gives you a competitive advantage because you'll know exactly what moves are legal and which ones put you at risk of losing through fouls.

Rules prevent injuries, ensure fairness, and create a level playing field where skill and strength determine the winner. Every professional competition follows strict rules, so mastering them is essential.

Building Your Arsenal

Raw strength isn't enough to dominate at armwrestling. You need:

  • Grip Strength: Your grip is your foundation. Develop a vice-like grip that never weakens.
  • Forearm Endurance: Matches can be long. Build forearm muscles that don't fatigue.
  • Wrist Power: Your wrists generate the curling and rotation power for techniques.
  • Shoulder & Arm Power: For toproll and pressing techniques, you need upper arm strength.
  • Hand Strength: Fingers and palm strength are critical for King's Move and hand dominance.
  • Technique Execution: The best strength in the world won't help if your technique is poor.
